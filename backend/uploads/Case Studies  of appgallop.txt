A telco-grade OMNI services platform that connected new digital service journeys with legacy OSS/BSS, CRM, ERP, billing, taxation, accounting, service assurance, support, logistics, and rule-management systems.

Challenge

Introducing a new platform into a legacy telco ecosystem
The platform had to integrate with core OSS/BSS, service desk, CRM, ERP, third-party applications, and telco operational systems.

Compliance requirements covered internal processes, external rules, accounting entries, tax, credit policy, service rules, SLA, and customer data handling.

The business needed a single window for supplier pricing, market pricing, service sales, invoicing, billing, collections, and support operations.

Solution

SOA-based enterprise platform with API-led service integration
AppGallop connected cloud, services, and subscription offering journeys with legacy telco systems through an SOA-based integration layer that reduced load on parent platforms.

The platform generated accounting entries for AR, AP, TDS, payments, reversals, settlements, partial refunds, credit notes, debit notes, customer segmentation, and credit-policy controls.

Support operations were integrated with NSD, SLA processes, customer-segment queues, ticket routing rules, auto/manual assignment, escalation paths, and service assurance workflows.

Outcomes

Automated customer onboarding and verification

GST, TDS, reversals, credit notes, and credit-policy compliance

Payment received and reconciliation workflows

Invoicing, billing, rating, charging, dunning, and split-invoice rules

Integrations across Enable Service, MACD, VAS, roster management, service assurance, and logistics

Unified operating visibility across supplier pricing, market pricing, sales, invoicing, collections, and support operations
